# Badges

Badges communicate status, category or risk.

## Types

- Status
- Feedback
- AI
- Risk
- Product

## WAI Risk status examples

- Draft
- Pending
- Processing
- Completed
- Cancelled
- Archived

## AI examples

- Generated
- Review required
- Validated

## Rules

- Badge text must be short.
- Color must be paired with text.
- Do not use badges as decoration.
