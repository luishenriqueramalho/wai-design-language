# Elevation

Elevation creates depth and visual hierarchy.

## Philosophy

Shadows are not decoration. They explain depth.

## Levels

### Level 1

Use for cards, widgets and resting surfaces.

### Level 2

Use for dropdowns, popovers, menus and floating panels.

### Level 3

Use for modals, drawers and command palettes.

## Rules

- Avoid colored shadows.
- Avoid heavy glow.
- Avoid excessive blur.
- Prefer subtle depth and clear contrast.
